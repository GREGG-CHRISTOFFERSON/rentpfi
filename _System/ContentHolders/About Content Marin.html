For more than 35 years, PFI’s Marin Apartment Homes has been one of the largest and leading multi-family housing providers in the county. We are focused on providing high quality apartment homes in some of the most desirable neighborhoods. Situated among abundant natural beauty and world class attractions, these communities offer an unparalleled lifestyle. Our apartment home locations currently include San Rafael and Novato, cities with high economic growth and supreme shopping, dining, and entertainment options.

Over 590 residential, multi-tenant units are being managed and maintained by a team of highly experienced staff members. Over 40 employees work tirelessly to ensure our tenants’ complete satisfaction.

Locally owned, the company has long established relationships with many local vendors supplying great quality equipment and appliances for our apartment communities. Our strong, reliable network of local businesses helps us deliver professional and timely services that meet all our tenants’ needs.

We pride ourselves for being an Industry leader, committed to continuous improvement through education, technology, and effective communication with our residents.
